Revive Foods

We fight food waste by recovering and repurposing edible surplus produce across the food supply chain.

#wastenot

Revive Foods is a mission-driven food company dedicated to eliminating food waste by recovering and repurposing edible surplus produce across the food supply chain.

We focus on recovering surplus fruit - often cosmetically challenged, overripe or bruised but perfectly edible - that would otherwise be wasted to create delicious, healthier and more affordable food products, starting with jam.

We envision a food system that maximizes all our resources - beginning on the farm and ending on your plate, and a world where our company's mission becomes the norm, and not the exception.
